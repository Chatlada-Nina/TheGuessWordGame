How to play

  • There are 10 words to guess. Press Play to start
  • You will see 2 images and a Hint for each guessing word.
  • Answer by filling in the answer and press OK button.
  • You will see the result of your answer. If it's correct the Score will increment but if it's incorrect, you will see the correct answer with the definition.
  • Click Next to keep playing and collecting your score.
  • Find out how many words you can guess, have fun!
